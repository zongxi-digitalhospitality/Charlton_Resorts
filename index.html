About us

Since 1949, Charltons have been welcoming guests with year-round accommodation and mountain-grown hospitality within our Rocky Mountain National Park hotels. Charltons Banff and the Royal Canadian Lodge are committed to providing exceptional visitor experiences. Our latest upgrades include extensive room renovations, aligned with our pledge to provide comfortable rooms and the most memorable guest stays for generations to come.
