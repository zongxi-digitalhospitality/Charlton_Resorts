Royal Canadian Lodge

The Rejuvenating Escape and Luxury Getaway You Have Been Waiting For

Stay with us at the Royal Canadian Lodge and surround yourself with unparalleled comfort and relaxation. Dine at our Evergreen Restaurant for a taste of fresh, local flavours blended with international flair, then dedicate some time to be pampered at our Grotto Spa. With 99 luxuriously modern guest rooms, we invite you to feel at home at the Royal Canadian Lodge, whether you are travelling solo, with family or colleagues, or are on a Banff romantic getaway.

Charltons Banff

Providing Boutique Charm & Rocky Mountain Hospitality for Over 60 Years

Experience the charm of our boutique, alpine style lodge, luxurious amenities, and complimentary services at Charltons Banff. One of few Banff hotels with a pool, hot tub, and steam room, we welcome you to truly relax. With 65 rooms, from spacious loft suites, to deluxe mountain view rooms, you will discover comfort, care, and thoughtful touches from the moment you check-in. Charltons Banff is known for exceptional service, and we look forward to welcoming you soon.
